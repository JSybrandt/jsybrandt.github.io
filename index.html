Ph.D. in CS. Focus in ML and NLP.

Justin completed a Ph.D. at Clemson University working under Dr. Ilya Safro. His thesis, Exploding Latent Features of Text and Graphs, explores embedding techniques and applications. This includes a focus in automatic hypothesis generation, wherein he developed the Moliere and Agatha systems. He has accepted a role with Google Brain in Pittsburgh, starting in August 2020.
