Academic Website

Sep 15, 2017

For the longest time I have been trying to figure out what is the best way to manage an academic website. So far I’ve had two major issues. Firstly, I don’t want to waste too much of my time setting the dang thing up. Secondly, I’m not sure what to say. Well all of that is going to change (it seems) thanks to this thing called Hugo and this fancy academic theme.

At the time of writing, I am a second year Ph.D. student, so there is a lot to figure out. That said, its been over a years worth of hair-pulling and number-crunching. So hopefully some of what comes out on this site will help someone out there. (Or at least, future employers will know how hard I tried).

Anyway, on this new platform I am going to attempt to work on writing with a more casual tone. I want to be able to present my work to both ivory tower elites as well as the typical layperson. So expect posts about machine learning, programming, technology, and surviving this wild ride we call grad school.
